About Automatify

Built by operators who speak nonprofit

Automatify was founded by a former nonprofit COO who spent a decade wrestling with siloed systems, grant deadlines, and staff burnout. After experiencing firsthand how much time and money was lost to manual processes, our team began building automation prototypes using Claude to streamline internal workflows. Those early experiments evolved into a dedicated automation studio focused solely on the needs of mission-driven organizations.

Today, our mission is simple: ensure that every nonprofit can operate with the sophistication of a modern tech company—without sacrificing its values or overwhelming its staff. We believe in transparent partnerships, ethical AI, and measurable outcomes. Every engagement is guided by three core values: respect for your mission, stewardship of your data, and a relentless focus on freeing your people to do the work only humans can do.

Nonprofit-first approach

We work only with nonprofits, foundations, and social enterprises, so every pattern we design is informed by real constraints like restricted grants, board oversight, and public accountability.
